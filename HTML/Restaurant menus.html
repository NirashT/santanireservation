Our Menus

Try some traditional local flavours! Over 15 Sri Lankan dishes are featured, including Black Pork Curry, Lagoon Crab Curry, Lamprais and Kottu, and family meals include the Godamba Roti Meal, Pittu Meal, Roast Paan with Crab Curry Meal and String Hopper Set Meal, among more. Traditional and contemporary combinations, as well as bites platters are available, to be shared with friends and family on any occasion.

 

Menu 1 description : "Gamey Rahata"

Boiled Manioc served with Fried Lake Fish, Dry Pork Black Curry, Scraped Coconut, Kochchi Sambol, Salt and Chilli Crusted Fried Malla Kola

 

Menu 2 description : "Yellow Rice Set Meal"

Yellow Rice served with Black Pork Curry or Chicken Curry, Potato Tempered, Brinjol Moju, Boiled Fried Egg, Dhal Curry, and Chutney

 

 

Menu 3 description : "Sri Lankan Noodle Family Meal"

Sri Lankan Style Vegetable Noodles accompanied with Boiled Egg and Potato Curry, Tempered Dhal Curry, Seeni Sambol and Chicken Red Curry with

Option of Dessert –Sago Pudding with Jaggery, Cashew Nuts and Raisins Or  Cherry Trifle
